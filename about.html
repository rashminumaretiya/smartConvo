This is SmartConvo

Why does this matter? Because we’re all customers at the end of the day. We believe that life improves when the customer journeys we encounter are seamless, efficient, and genuinely helpful.

The motivation behind the creation of SmartConvo

We created SmartConvo to tackle the reliability challenges that businesses face with GenAI and large language models (LLMs) in their operations. Our goal is to eliminate hallucinations in GenAI projects and help enterprises achieve maximum accuracy.

The root of the accuracy issues lies not in the technology itself, which excels in creativity and text manipulation, but in the quality of the training data. Our solution, CogniSwitch, enhances enterprise data by converting it into a multidimensional format stored in a knowledge graph, making it suitable for LLMs and ensuring context retention for accurate results.
